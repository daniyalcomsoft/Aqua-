Additives

AquaBird mineral water contains minerals, such as calcium, magnesium, and potassium, which are essential for good health. These minerals are usually acquired through the water as it passes through underground rock formations, where it picks up the minerals. AquaBird guarantees safe, pure, and healthy composition

Beneifits

AquaBird Mineral water has health benefits, such as improved digestion, better hydration, and increased bone strength. At AquaBird we use a reverse osmosis purification system in which water is forced under pressure through the membrane, which allows water molecules to pass through but blocks contaminants such as dissolved salts, bacteria, and viruses.

Chemical Composition

At AquaBird we make sure that your drinking water is tested regularly for contaminants, and measures such as filtration or disinfection are taken regularly for safe, pure, and healthy chemical composition.
